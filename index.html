Eslaforde Simple Solutions builds interactive apps that give you a tactile workspace to explore anagrams and spelling bee puzzles — not auto-solvers, but thinking tools.

Simple Anagram Helper

A hands-on workspace for rearranging letters. Enter your letters and get draggable tiles you can move between boxes and a freeform workspace to work through anagram puzzles at your own pace.

  • Drag tiles between ordered boxes and a freeform workspace
  • Shuffle tiles to spark new ideas and see fresh arrangements
  • Lock letters in place once you're sure of their position
  • Resize the workspace to suit your solving style
  • Fill all boxes in one tap, or work tile by tile
Download on the App Store

Simple Spelling Bee Helper

A visual workspace for spelling bee puzzles. Enter your 7 letters — with the required centre letter highlighted in gold — and get hexagonal tiles you can drag, group, and shuffle to discover words.

  • Hexagonal tiles inspired by the classic spelling bee format
  • Drag individual tiles or select and move groups at once
  • Fix tiles in place and shuffle the rest around them
  • Adjustable shuffle zones to focus on specific letter combos
  • Add duplicate tiles for letters that repeat in words
  • Works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac
