Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026
Developer: Eslaforde LLC

Overview

Eslaforde LLC ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile applications ("Apps"), including Simple Anagram Helper and Simple Spelling Bee Helper.

Information We Collect

Our Apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information through our Apps.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools within our Apps.
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies.
  • All processing occurs locally on your device.

Data Storage

Simple Anagram Helper stores your current puzzle state (entered letters and tile positions) locally on your device using standard iOS preferences. Simple Spelling Bee Helper stores puzzle state in memory during your session only — it is not persisted when the app is terminated. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Subscriptions

Our Apps offer optional auto-renewable subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not process payments directly and do not have access to your payment information. Subscription status is verified locally on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media SDKs. We do not share data with any third parties because we do not collect data in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Our Apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we do not collect any personal data, our Apps are safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
